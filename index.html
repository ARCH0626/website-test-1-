Our Story

A Legacy of Royal Indian Hospitality

The Beginning of an Affair

Founded in 1999 by visionaries Priyank Sukhija and Y.P. Ashok, Lazeez Affaire emerged as one of Delhi's pioneering fine-dining establishments, bringing the opulence of Mughal courts to modern India.

Our name speaks to a passionate relationship with authentic flavours, where every dish is a love letter to India's rich culinary heritage. From the velvety galouti kebabs to the aromatic biryanis, each recipe has been perfected over decades.

1999

Lazeez Affaire opens its first location in Delhi, introducing luxury North Indian dining to the capital.

2005

Recognized as one of India's Top 50 Restaurants by leading food critics and publications.

2015

Expanded to multiple locations across Delhi NCR, maintaining unwavering quality standards.

2025

Celebrating 26 years of culinary excellence and serving over 2 million satisfied guests.

Today, Lazeez Affaire stands as a testament to timeless hospitality, where tradition meets contemporary luxury in every aspect‚Äîfrom our palatial interiors to our meticulously crafted menu.
